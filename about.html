Our mission, vision, and values

Mission

Improve quality of life through conservation

We deliver community-led initiatives to protect marine ecosystems and support sustainable livelihoods.

Vision

Values

We work with stakeholders at every level to achieve lasting, measurable outcomes.

Our journey

PATTE Conservation emerged from the urgent need to restore coral reef health and secure sustainable futures for coastal communities.

As pressures on marine habitats increased, local leaders, fishers, and youth united to build a framework for stewardship. Together, we initiated restoration, monitoring, and education programs that empower communities to care for the ocean they depend on.

Food security

Better fisheries management and habitat recovery to sustain nutrition.

Economic development

Eco‑tourism and ocean-friendly enterprises that create dignified work.

Marine conservation

Effective biodiversity protection through LMMAs and co‑management.

Sustainable resources

Long-term viability of marine ecosystems for generations to come.
